Time Travel

“Sinai Mountains, Egypt”

Acrylic on canvas · ∅ 30 cm · 2024

Part of an ongoing series, this work imagines the first arrival of celestial visitors to Earth, seen through a spacecraft window.

The Sinai Mountains were later transformed into one of humanity’s sacred geographies, becoming associated with divine encounters, revelation and the origins of religious tradition. Here, however, they are presented in an earlier moment, before history and civilization.
